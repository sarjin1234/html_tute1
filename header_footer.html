Check out my beloved girl:

Ai Haibara, real name Shiho Miyano, is the inventor of the APTX 4869 poison 
that shrunk Shinichi Kudo into Conan Edogawa, and a victim of the poison herself. 
Formerly Sherry of the Black Organization, she is on the run from them and lives with 
Professor Agasa. She is also one of few characters who knows Shinichi is Conan.
